* Use a stack for all code quoting
  * Quote simple statement
    * top.add('_mpy.top.add(parse("..."))')
  * Quote suite (non-macro)
    * top.add('_mpy.push()')
    * for s in body: s.quote()
    * top.add('_mpy.top.add(Suite(self.header, _mpy.pop())))
  * Suite quoting (macro):
    * push()
    * for s in body: s.quote()
    * top.add(Suite(self.header, pop()))
